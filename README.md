## Backstory 
I wanted to have fun building a game and due to the restrictions of a 8x8 matrix, I tought snake would be a great choice. The problem with snake is that it's becoming boring pretty fast, so I managed to do some "twists" to it, which increase the enjoyment of the game. ğŸ

## Game description
It's pretty straight forward. You are controlling a snake and need to eat the blinking food without touching the wall or eating yourself.

Here comes 4 level of difficulty:
- <b>Difficulty 0</b> - nothing added, everything is classic.
- <b>Difficulty 1</b> - food has a <b>45% chance</b> of being poisoned. You can see that by the on/off yellow ledğŸ’¡. If you eat a poisoned food, the snake is being reversed (head and tail swapped). 
<br>
*Keep an eye on the tail*ğŸ˜… 
- <b>Difficulty 3</b> - walls are dynamically generated on the map and you should avoid them.

- <b>Difficulty 4</b> - food can be poisoned and your snake is dizzy ğŸ’« (your controls are reversed: RIGHT <-> LEFT and UP <-> DOWN). 
<br>
*You should be very focused for that*
